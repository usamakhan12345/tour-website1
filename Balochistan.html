Hanna Lake

The Hannah Lake is located in Quetta the capital of Balochistan. This beautiful lake is frozen and frosted in winters. Many tourists from the country come to visit the lake to explore the beauty and charming sight. Hanna Lake is located at a distance of 18Km from the city of Quetta.

Kund Malir Beach

If you are looking for breathtaking exotic beaches in Balochistan, Kund Malir definitely takes the lead as one of the most famous beach attractions in the country. This tourist spot has attracted a lot of tourists from Karachi and other places in the past couple of years.

Waadi-e-Bolan

Waadi-e-Bolan is located in Bolan, Balochistan. This place stretches along the mountains as well as blue waters running in-between. Waadi-e-Bolan is the place where the British set up the first railway system of Pakistan.

Pir Ghayb Waterfalls

The Pir Ghayb Waterfalls are located in Bolan, Balochistan. Local residents believe that the falls initiated when an invisible saint struck a stick in the mountain when he was attacked by some enemies of his time. The place represents a mesmerizing view of the place.
